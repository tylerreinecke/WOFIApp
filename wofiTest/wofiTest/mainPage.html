Adventure around the Bay, be inspired by art in magnificent locations, learn and have free fun < All-in-one

WELCOME

How fortunate we are to have the San Diego Bay as part of our local landscape. Once you get seaside, it becomes a world of blue skies and bluer water strung with more than a hundred intriguing pieces of art.

How many pieces have you seen? Is there a sculpture or a mural closest to where you live? So much to see, so many ways to explore and even more ways to enjoy. It’s like having a giant playground outside your door. Pick a tour or a location, plan a way to enjoy the art and take the San Diego Bay Art Tour app with you. You’re off for good times and a fun adventure.
